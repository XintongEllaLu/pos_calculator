Methodology
Data Provenance & Analytical Framework
Transparent disclosure of data sources, definitions, model architecture, and limitations. Review before applying PoS estimates in investment models.
01

Data Source

AACT (Aggregate Analysis of ClinicalTrials.gov), curated by CTTI with FDA support. Covers 480,000+ registered studies. Monthly static snapshots used for reproducibility.

02

Success Definition

A trial is classified as successful if overall_status = Completed. Terminated or Withdrawn trials are failures. Trials stopped for external non-scientific reasons are excluded to preserve signal integrity.

03

Statistical Method

Phase-level PoS uses Wilson score confidence intervals. The calculator applies logistic regression with phase, area, modality, sponsor, design, and log-transformed enrolment as predictors.

04

Area Classification

Disease areas assigned via MeSH term matching from the AACT conditions table. Each trial maps to the highest-priority bucket from a ranked keyword hierarchy across 9 therapeutic areas.

05

Scope & Eligibility

Interventional studies only. Phases 1, 2, 3 and combinations (1/2, 2/3). Trials with enrollment > 0 and registration date 2005–2025. Cohort spans all sponsor types.

06

Limitations

Registration was inconsistent before 2008. Trial completion ≠ drug approval. Sponsor quality, protocol nuances, and competitive landscape are not captured. For analytical purposes only — not investment advice.
