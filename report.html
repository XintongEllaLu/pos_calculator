Section VI
Methodology & Data Documentation

Transparent disclosure of data provenance, analytical definitions, model architecture, and known limitations. This section should be reviewed prior to using PoS estimates in investment or portfolio models.

Data Source

AACT (Aggregate Analysis of ClinicalTrials.gov), curated by CTTI with support from the FDA and pharmaceutical industry. Covers 480,000+ registered studies. Monthly static snapshots used for reproducibility.

Success Definition

A trial is classified as successful if overall_status = Completed. Terminated or Withdrawn trials are classified as failures. Trials stopped for external, non-scientific reasons (COVID-19, funding loss, strategic business decisions) are excluded from the analysis.

Statistical Method

Phase-level PoS uses Wilson score confidence intervals, which are robust near boundary probabilities and with small samples. The calculator uses logistic regression with phase, area, modality, sponsor, design, and log-transformed enrolment as predictors.

Therapeutic Area Classification

Disease areas are assigned via MeSH (Medical Subject Headings) term matching from the AACT conditions table. Each trial is assigned to the highest-priority matching bucket from a ranked keyword hierarchy covering 9 therapeutic areas.

Scope & Eligibility Criteria

Interventional studies only. Phases 1, 2, 3, and combinations (1/2, 2/3). Trials with enrollment > 0 and a registered start date between 2005 and 2025 are included. The cohort spans all sponsor types.

Limitations & Disclaimers

Registration on ClinicalTrials.gov was inconsistent prior to 2008. Trial completion does not equal drug approval. Sponsor quality, protocol design nuances, and competitive landscape are not captured. This tool is for analytical purposes only — not investment advice.
